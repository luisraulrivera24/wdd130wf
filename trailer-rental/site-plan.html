Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my site is to provide trailer rentals. These rentals are for people who need to dispose of of any form of trash and need the proper equipment to do so since it is large amounts of trash, which can be difficult to transport.

Audience

The target audience is people that need to dispose of trash. This can be from doing remodeling in their homes, or old junk in their garage. These trailer rentals are for people who do not have trailers and need them to dispose of the trash they have in their homes.

Content

Home page

Intro

As a dedicated family business, Rivera’s Trailer Rentals has made it its mission to provide its customers with the appropriate trailer to support our customers with getting rid of junk as they remodel their homes, get rid of old household items, etc. We want to help! Our business not only provides your dump trailer needs, but we help you every step of the way by dropping off our trailers to the specified location given by the customer and picking up the trailer when the time is ready to get rid of the trash! We dispose of the trash properly for our customers!

Questions Answered:

For those visiting our website, we expect the following questions to be answered:

• What type of trailers are provided?
• What are the prices for renting the trailers?
• Do I have to pick it up? Or does the company drop it off?
• How does the trailer get returned?
• What if I need the trailer longer than I reserved it for?
• Does the company load all the trash for me?
• What are the business hours?
• Where can I search for any potential job opportunities?
• What are the qualifications for the job I am interested in?

Contact Information

Our contact information can be found on every webpage in the footer section as well as in the “Careers” tab. If a customer prefers to chat with us online, they can reach one of our representatives online through our built-in chat. Our customer representatives are always available to help.

We value our customers and want to provide the support we can with getting rid of things they no longer need! Whether it is from old furniture, remodeling, non-operable items that need to get thrown away, the list goes on! With our competitive prices and the support we provide for our customers by dropping off and picking up our trailers, we set our customers up for success. We don’t want our customers to deal with the hassle of finding where to throw away all the trash. All that is required in the customer’s end is to book a trailer online. We are in charge of the rest! We are in constant communication with our customers if there is any need or an issue arises. The simpler the experience, the smoother the process will be for our customers. For any further inquiries, check our website out or feel free to contact us.

Trailers & Prices

Trailer Options and Prices

Those visiting our site will want to head on over to the “Trailers & Prices” tab which will automatically takes them to all the available trailers that they can rent from us, along with a break down of pricing per day for each trailer rental (trailer configurations, prices, dimensions, and availability). When customers find what they like, they can click on the “Reserve Now” button to see the availability of that trailer and when they can get their hands on one.

Making a Reservation

When booking a reservation online, the following information will be needed:

1. Full Name
2. Date of Birth
3. A form of ID
4. Credit/Debit card
5. Address

Careers

Career Options

For those interested in finding a job with “Rivera’s Trailer Rentals” the “Careers” tab displays a list of the different positions to apply to, as well as the availability status for each individual position. All that is needed is to go to the “Careers” tab, look at the position of interest, read more about the role and responsibilities, and checking to see the availability of that role.

If interested in any of the positions, please submit your resume with name and phone number to the following email address: applications@RiveraTrailerRentals.com.
